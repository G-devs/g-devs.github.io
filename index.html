  1. Smartwatch (Atmega1284p and HC-08 BLE based. PCB , Gerber files ready.) |

    2. It'll be basically a Bluetooth based Smartwatch that will surely have the features like a normal Watch as well but also have features such as listed below :

    1. Pedometer for calculating steps and Calories burned
    2. WhatsApp, Missed calls, SMS, E-Mail notifications
    3. Gyroscope (In order to wake the screen)
    4. Blood Pressure Indicator
    5. Ambient Light Sensor
    6. Vibration

    (PCB Design for the SmartWatch)
